Professor Daniel Nweke Nwokoye

1939 - 2011

About Daddy's life

  • 1964 - 1968 Professor Nwokoye was educated at the University College, London where he obtained B.Sc. (Lond.) 1964 and Ph.D (Lond.) 1968. He also had an Industrial experience with Rendel Panal and Tritton (Lond.).
  • 1968 - 1970 He had 2 years post doctorial fellowship at the University College, London.
  • 1970 - 1974 He was a lecturer at the Polytechnic of Central London (now University of Westminster).
  • 1974 - 1977 He joined the University of Benin in May 1974. In September 1977, he became a Professor of Civil Engineering in the University of Benin, Benin city, Nigeria.
  • 1978 - 1982 He was the first Dean of Faculty of Engineering, University of Benin.
  • 1989 - 1990 From November 1989 to December 1990, he was the first National Vice- President of the Nigerian Institute of Structural Engineers. He was re-elected in December 1990.
  • 1991 - 1994 He was the National President of the Nigerian Institution of Structural Engineers (re-elected 1992 and 1993).
  • Memberships He was a Fellow of the Institution of Civil Engineers (United Kingdom), a Fellow of the Institute Structural Engineers (United Kingdom), a fellow of the Institute of Wood Science (United Kingdom), a Fellow of the Nigerian Institution of Structural Engineers, a fellow of the Nigerian Society of Engineers, registered Engineer (COREN) a Chartered Civil Engineer and a Chartered Structural Engineer.
  • Awards He was the winner, Institution of Structural Engineers (United Kingdom) GUTHRIE BROWN AWARD AND PREMIUM for the session 1974/75.
  • Personal Life: He was a loving and devoted husband, father and grand-father. He loved his family unconditionally. A kind-hearted and principled man, a devout christian who was devoted to God with an unshakable faith despite the lemons thrown to him by the world. He had great love for others. He loved and prayed for everyone despite their personalities or flaws. He was indeed an extremely rare gem. Heaven would have been so excited to have him.
